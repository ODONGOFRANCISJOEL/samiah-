About Us

Your Premium Travel Partner

Shamiah Touring Company has been providing luxury transportation services since 2010. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service with the highest standards of safety, comfort, and reliability.

Our fleet of late-model Mercedes vehicles is meticulously maintained to ensure your journey is as smooth as possible. From airport transfers to corporate events, weddings to city tours, we cater to all your transportation needs.

Our professional chauffeurs are trained to provide discreet, courteous service while ensuring you reach your destination on time, every time.
